### Ingredients

<RecipeIngredients>
  <>Pizza Dough (store-bought or homemade) (1 piece)</>
  <>Tomato Sauce (1/2 cup)</>
  <>Fresh Mozzarella, sliced (4 oz)</>
  <>Ripe Tomatoes, sliced (1-2 pieces)</>
  <>Fresh Basil Leaves</>
  <>Olive Oil (1 tbsp)</>
  <>Salt & Pepper to taste</>
</RecipeIngredients>

### Instructions

<RecipeInstructions>
  <RecipeInstructions.Section title="Prepare Pizza">
    <RecipeInstructions.Step>
      Preheat oven to <strong>475°F (245°C)</strong>.
    </RecipeInstructions.Step>
    <RecipeInstructions.Step>
      Roll out <strong>Dough</strong> on a floured surface and transfer to a baking sheet.
    </RecipeInstructions.Step>
    <RecipeInstructions.Step>
      Spread <strong>Tomato Sauce</strong> evenly over dough.
    </RecipeInstructions.Step>
    <RecipeInstructions.Step>
      Top with <strong>Mozzarella</strong> and <strong>Tomato Slices</strong>.
    </RecipeInstructions.Step>
    <RecipeInstructions.Step>
      Drizzle with <strong>Olive Oil</strong>, season with <strong>Salt</strong> and <strong>Pepper</strong>.
    </RecipeInstructions.Step>
    <RecipeInstructions.Step>
      Bake for <strong>12-15 minutes</strong> until crust is golden and cheese is bubbly.
    </RecipeInstructions.Step>
    <RecipeInstructions.Step>
      Top with fresh <strong>Basil</strong> before serving.
    </RecipeInstructions.Step>
  </RecipeInstructions.Section>
</RecipeInstructions>

### Notes

<RecipeNotes>
  <>For best results, use a pizza stone if available</>
  <>Can add other toppings like olives or mushrooms</>
  <>Let dough come to room temperature before rolling for easier handling</>
