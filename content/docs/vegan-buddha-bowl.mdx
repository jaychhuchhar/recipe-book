## Ingredients
- 1 cup cooked quinoa or brown rice
- 1 cup chickpeas, drained
- 1 cup broccoli florets
- 1 cup sweet potato, cubed
- 1 avocado, sliced
- 1 cup mixed greens
- 2 tbsp tahini
- 1 tbsp lemon juice
- Salt & pepper to taste

## Instructions
1. Roast sweet potato and broccoli at 400°F (200°C) for 20 minutes.
2. Arrange all ingredients in a bowl.
3. Whisk tahini, lemon juice, salt, and pepper for dressing. Drizzle over bowl.
