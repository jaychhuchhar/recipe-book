## Ingredients
- Room Temperature Butter (1 Cup)
- Powdered Sugar (1.5 Cup)
- All Purpose Flour (3 Cup)
- Egg (2 Nos)
- Baking Powder (2 Tsp)
- Salt (0.5 Tsp)
- Vanilla Extract (2 Tsp)
- Chocolate Chips (1 Heartful)

## Instructions

### Prepare Dry Ingredients
1. In a mixing bowl, add All Purpose Flour, Baking Powder, and Salt and mix thoroughly.

### Prepare Wet Ingredients
2. In a mixing bowl, add Butter and Sugar, mix it well.
3. Add 1 egg at a time mixing it thoroughly.
4. Add Vanilla Extract and mix well.

### Combine and Bake
5. Add Dry Mix, 1/3 at a time mixing it thoroughly.
6. Once everything is mixed properly, add Chocolate Chips and fold in the mixture.
7. Make medium sized cookies and put them on baking paper over a baking tray.
8. Preheat the oven at 180â„ƒ and bake 5 to 6 medium cookies at a time for 15 min to 17 min.

## Notes
- Storage: Do not refrigerate. Keep it in cool, air tight and dry place.
- Use hand for mixing for better result.
- Use rotating plate for even cooking if using conventional oven in microwave.
