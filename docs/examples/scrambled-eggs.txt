Title: Simple Scrambled Eggs
Description: Perfect scrambled eggs in minutes
Category: Breakfast
Servings: 2
Prep Time: 2 Minutes
Cook Time: 3 Minutes

## Ingredients
- 4 large eggs
- 2 tablespoons butter
- Salt and pepper to taste
- 2 tablespoons milk or cream

## Instructions
1. Crack eggs into a bowl and whisk with milk, salt, and pepper
2. Heat butter in a non-stick pan over medium-low heat
3. Pour in eggs and let sit for 20 seconds
4. Gently stir with a spatula, pushing eggs from edges to center
5. Continue stirring gently until eggs are just set but still creamy
6. Remove from heat and serve immediately

## Notes
- Low heat is key for creamy eggs
- Don't overcook - eggs continue cooking off heat
- Serve on warm plates
