### Ingredients

<RecipeIngredients>
  <>Milk (dairy or plant-based) (1 cup)</>
  <>Turmeric (1/2 tsp)</>
  <>Cinnamon (1/4 tsp)</>
  <>Black Pepper (pinch)</>
  <>Honey or Maple Syrup (1 tsp)</>
</RecipeIngredients>

### Instructions

<RecipeInstructions>
  <RecipeInstructions.Section title="Prepare Golden Milk">
    <RecipeInstructions.Step>
      Heat <strong>Milk</strong> and spices in a saucepan until warm.
    </RecipeInstructions.Step>
    <RecipeInstructions.Step>
      Sweeten to taste with <strong>Honey</strong> or <strong>Maple Syrup</strong>. Whisk and serve.
    </RecipeInstructions.Step>
  </RecipeInstructions.Section>
</RecipeInstructions>

### Notes

<RecipeNotes>
  <>Black pepper helps with turmeric absorption</>
  <>Can add ginger for extra warmth and flavor</>
  <>Store spice blend in advance for quick preparation</>
</RecipeNotes>
