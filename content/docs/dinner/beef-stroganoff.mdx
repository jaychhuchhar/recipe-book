### Ingredients

<RecipeIngredients>
  <>Beef Sirloin, cut into strips (1.5 lbs)</>
  <>Egg Noodles (8 oz)</>
  <>Mushrooms, sliced (8 oz)</>
  <>Large Onion, sliced (1 piece)</>
  <>Garlic, minced (3 cloves)</>
  <>All-purpose Flour (2 tbsp)</>
  <>Beef Broth (2 cups)</>
  <>Sour Cream (1 cup)</>
  <>Tomato Paste (2 tbsp)</>
  <>Butter (3 tbsp)</>
  <>Vegetable Oil (2 tbsp)</>
  <>Paprika (1 tsp)</>
  <>Fresh Parsley for garnish</>
  <>Salt and Pepper to taste</>
</RecipeIngredients>

### Instructions

<RecipeInstructions>
  <RecipeInstructions.Section title="Prepare Base">
    <RecipeInstructions.Step>
      Cook <strong>Egg Noodles</strong> according to package directions, drain and set aside.
    </RecipeInstructions.Step>
    <RecipeInstructions.Step>
      Season <strong>Beef Strips</strong> with <strong>Salt</strong> and <strong>Pepper</strong>.
    </RecipeInstructions.Step>
    <RecipeInstructions.Step>
      Heat <strong>Oil</strong> in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Brown <strong>Beef Strips</strong>, then remove.
    </RecipeInstructions.Step>
  </RecipeInstructions.Section>
  <RecipeInstructions.Section title="Make Sauce">
    <RecipeInstructions.Step>
      In the same pan, melt <strong>Butter</strong> and saut√© <strong>Onions</strong> and <strong>Mushrooms</strong> until soft.
    </RecipeInstructions.Step>
    <RecipeInstructions.Step>
      Add <strong>Garlic</strong> and cook <strong>1 minute</strong>. Sprinkle <strong>Flour</strong> over vegetables and stir.
    </RecipeInstructions.Step>
    <RecipeInstructions.Step>
      Gradually add <strong>Beef Broth</strong>, whisking to prevent lumps.
    </RecipeInstructions.Step>
    <RecipeInstructions.Step>
      Stir in <strong>Tomato Paste</strong> and <strong>Paprika</strong>. Return <strong>Beef</strong> to pan.
    </RecipeInstructions.Step>
    <RecipeInstructions.Step>
      Simmer <strong>10 minutes</strong> until sauce thickens and beef is tender.
    </RecipeInstructions.Step>
    <RecipeInstructions.Step>
      Remove from heat, stir in <strong>Sour Cream</strong>.
    </RecipeInstructions.Step>
    <RecipeInstructions.Step>
      Serve over <strong>Egg Noodles</strong>, garnish with fresh <strong>Parsley</strong>.
    </RecipeInstructions.Step>
  </RecipeInstructions.Section>
</RecipeInstructions>

### Notes
