### Ingredients

<RecipeIngredients>
  <>Boneless, Skinless Chicken Breasts (4 pieces)</>
  <>All-purpose Flour (1 cup)</>
  <>Eggs, beaten (2 pieces)</>
  <>Panko Breadcrumbs (2 cups)</>
  <>Grated Parmesan Cheese (1/2 cup)</>
  <>Marinara Sauce (2 cups)</>
  <>Fresh Mozzarella, sliced (8 oz)</>
  <>Fresh Basil, chopped (1/4 cup)</>
  <>Olive Oil (1/4 cup)</>
  <>Salt and Pepper to taste</>
  <>Pasta for serving (optional)</>
</RecipeIngredients>

### Instructions

<RecipeInstructions>
  <RecipeInstructions.Section title="Prepare Chicken">
    <RecipeInstructions.Step>
      Preheat oven to <strong>425°F (220°C)</strong>.
    </RecipeInstructions.Step>
    <RecipeInstructions.Step>
      Pound <strong>Chicken Breasts</strong> to <strong>1/2 inch thickness</strong>. Season with <strong>Salt</strong> and <strong>Pepper</strong>.
    </RecipeInstructions.Step>
    <RecipeInstructions.Step>
      Set up breading station: <strong>Flour</strong> in one dish, <strong>Beaten Eggs</strong> in another, and <strong>Breadcrumbs</strong> mixed with <strong>Parmesan</strong> in a third.
    </RecipeInstructions.Step>
    <RecipeInstructions.Step>
      Dredge each chicken breast in <strong>Flour</strong>, then <strong>Egg</strong>, then <strong>Breadcrumb Mixture</strong>.
    </RecipeInstructions.Step>
  </RecipeInstructions.Section>
  <RecipeInstructions.Section title="Cook and Bake">
    <RecipeInstructions.Step>
      Heat <strong>Olive Oil</strong> in a large oven-safe skillet over medium-high heat.
    </RecipeInstructions.Step>
    <RecipeInstructions.Step>
      Cook chicken <strong>3-4 minutes per side</strong> until golden brown.
    </RecipeInstructions.Step>
    <RecipeInstructions.Step>
      Top each piece with <strong>Marinara Sauce</strong> and <strong>Mozzarella Slices</strong>.
    </RecipeInstructions.Step>
    <RecipeInstructions.Step>
      Transfer skillet to oven and bake <strong>15-20 minutes</strong> until chicken reaches <strong>165°F</strong> internal temperature.
    </RecipeInstructions.Step>
    <RecipeInstructions.Step>
      Garnish with fresh <strong>Basil</strong> and serve immediately.
    </RecipeInstructions.Step>
  </RecipeInstructions.Section>
</RecipeInstructions>

### Notes

<RecipeNotes>
  <>For extra crispy coating, let breaded chicken rest 10 minutes before cooking</>
  <>Can be served over pasta, with a side salad, or on crusty bread as a sandwich</>
  <>Make ahead tip: bread chicken in the morning and refrigerate until ready to cook</>
</RecipeNotes>
