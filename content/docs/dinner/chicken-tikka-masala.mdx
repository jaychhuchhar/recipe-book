### Ingredients

<RecipeIngredients>
  <>Boneless Chicken Breast, cubed (1 lb)</>
  <>Plain Yogurt (1/2 cup)</>
  <>Lemon Juice (2 tbsp)</>
  <>Garam Masala (2 tsp)</>
  <>Turmeric (1 tsp)</>
  <>Cumin (1 tsp)</>
  <>Paprika (1 tsp)</>
  <>Onion, chopped (1 piece)</>
  <>Garlic, minced (2 cloves)</>
  <>Ginger, grated (1 tbsp)</>
  <>Tomato Sauce (1 can, 14 oz)</>
  <>Heavy Cream (1/2 cup)</>
  <>Butter (2 tbsp)</>
  <>Salt & Pepper to taste</>
</RecipeIngredients>

### Instructions

<RecipeInstructions>
  <RecipeInstructionsSection title="Marinate Chicken">
    <RecipeInstructionsStep>
      Marinate <strong>Chicken</strong> in <strong>Yogurt</strong>, <strong>Lemon Juice</strong>, and half the spices for <strong>30 minutes</strong>.
    </RecipeInstructionsStep>
  </RecipeInstructionsSection>
  <RecipeInstructionsSection title="Cook Curry">
    <RecipeInstructionsStep>
      Saut√© <strong>Onion</strong>, <strong>Garlic</strong>, and <strong>Ginger</strong> in <strong>Butter</strong> until soft.
    </RecipeInstructionsStep>
    <RecipeInstructionsStep>
      Add remaining spices, cook <strong>1 minute</strong>.
    </RecipeInstructionsStep>
    <RecipeInstructionsStep>
      Add <strong>Chicken</strong> and cook until browned.
    </RecipeInstructionsStep>
    <RecipeInstructionsStep>
      Stir in <strong>Tomato Sauce</strong>, simmer <strong>20 minutes</strong>.
    </RecipeInstructionsStep>
    <RecipeInstructionsStep>
      Add <strong>Cream</strong>, simmer <strong>10 more minutes</strong>. Serve with rice or naan.
    </RecipeInstructionsStep>
  </RecipeInstructionsSection>
</RecipeInstructions>

### Notes

  <>Adjust spice levels to taste preference</>
  <>Serve with basmati rice and fresh naan bread</>
