Title: Homemade Bread
Description: Simple no-knead bread that anyone can make
Category: Snacks
Cuisine: European
Difficulty: Easy
Servings: 8
Prep Time: 15 Minutes
Cook Time: 45 Minutes
Calories: 180
Dietary: Vegetarian
Cost: $2

## Ingredients
- 3 cups all-purpose flour
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1/2 teaspoon active dry yeast
- 1 5/8 cups warm water
- 1 tablespoon olive oil

## Instructions

### Mix Dough
1. In a large bowl, whisk together flour, salt, and yeast
   Alt: Dry ingredients mixed in bowl
2. Add water and olive oil, stir until a shaggy dough forms
   Alt: Shaggy wet dough in bowl
3. Cover bowl with plastic wrap and let rise for 12-18 hours at room temperature
   Alt: Covered bowl with risen bubbly dough

### Shape and Bake
4. Preheat oven to 450Â°F with a Dutch oven inside
   Alt: Empty Dutch oven heating in oven
5. Turn dough onto floured surface and fold it over itself a few times
   Alt: Hands folding dough on floured surface
6. Shape into a rough ball and place seam-side down on parchment paper
   Alt: Round dough ball on parchment paper
7. Carefully place in hot Dutch oven, cover, and bake 30 minutes
   Alt: Bread dough in covered Dutch oven
8. Remove lid and bake 15 more minutes until golden brown
   Alt: Golden brown crusty bread loaf

## Notes
- The dough will be very sticky - this is normal
- Use parchment paper to transfer dough safely
- Let bread cool completely before slicing
- Store in airtight container for up to 5 days
