### Ingredients

<RecipeIngredients>
  <>Ripe Bananas, mashed (3 pieces)</>
  <>Melted Butter (1/3 cup)</>
  <>Sugar (3/4 cup)</>
  <>Egg, beaten (1 piece)</>
  <>Vanilla Extract (1 tsp)</>
  <>Baking Soda (1 tsp)</>
  <>Salt (pinch)</>
  <>All-purpose Flour (1 1/2 cups)</>
  <>Chopped Walnuts (optional) (1/2 cup)</>
  <>Cinnamon (1/2 tsp)</>
</RecipeIngredients>

### Instructions

<RecipeInstructions>
  <RecipeInstructionsSection title="Prepare Batter">
    <RecipeInstructionsStep>
      Preheat oven to <strong>350°F (175°C)</strong>. Grease a <strong>9x5 inch loaf pan</strong>.
    </RecipeInstructionsStep>
    <RecipeInstructionsStep>
      In a large bowl, mix <strong>Mashed Bananas</strong> and <strong>Melted Butter</strong>.
    </RecipeInstructionsStep>
    <RecipeInstructionsStep>
      Stir in <strong>Sugar</strong>, <strong>Beaten Egg</strong>, and <strong>Vanilla Extract</strong>.
    </RecipeInstructionsStep>
    <RecipeInstructionsStep>
      Sprinkle <strong>Baking Soda</strong> and <strong>Salt</strong> over mixture and stir.
    </RecipeInstructionsStep>
    <RecipeInstructionsStep>
      Add <strong>Flour</strong> and <strong>Cinnamon</strong>, mix until just combined (don't overmix).
    </RecipeInstructionsStep>
    <RecipeInstructionsStep>
      Fold in <strong>Walnuts</strong> if using.
    </RecipeInstructionsStep>
  </RecipeInstructionsSection>
  <RecipeInstructionsSection title="Bake">
    <RecipeInstructionsStep>
      Pour batter into prepared <strong>Loaf Pan</strong>.
    </RecipeInstructionsStep>
    <RecipeInstructionsStep>
      Bake for <strong>55-65 minutes</strong> until a toothpick inserted in center comes out clean.
    </RecipeInstructionsStep>
    <RecipeInstructionsStep>
      Cool in pan for <strong>10 minutes</strong>, then turn out onto wire rack.
    </RecipeInstructionsStep>
  </RecipeInstructionsSection>
</RecipeInstructions>

### Notes

<RecipeNotes>
  <>Use very ripe bananas with brown spots for best flavor</>
  <>Don't overmix the batter or bread will be tough</>
  <>Can add chocolate chips, blueberries, or other mix-ins</>
  <>Wrapped bread stays fresh for 3-4 days at room temperature</>
</RecipeNotes>
