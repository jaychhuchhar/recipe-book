## Ingredients
- 1 pizza dough (store-bought or homemade)
- 1/2 cup tomato sauce
- 4 oz fresh mozzarella, sliced
- 1-2 ripe tomatoes, sliced
- Fresh basil leaves
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- Salt & pepper to taste

## Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 475°F (245°C).
2. Roll out dough on a floured surface and transfer to a baking sheet.
3. Spread tomato sauce evenly over dough.
4. Top with mozzarella and tomato slices.
5. Drizzle with olive oil, season with salt and pepper.
6. Bake for 12-15 minutes until crust is golden and cheese is bubbly.
7. Top with fresh basil before serving.
