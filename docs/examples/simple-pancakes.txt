Title: Simple Pancakes
Description: Fluffy pancakes perfect for weekend breakfast
Category: Breakfast
Cuisine: American
Difficulty: Easy
Servings: 4
Prep Time: 10 Minutes
Cook Time: 15 Minutes
Calories: 280
Dietary: Vegetarian
Cost: $4

## Ingredients
- 2 cups all-purpose flour
- 2 tablespoons sugar
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 2 large eggs
- 1 3/4 cups milk
- 1/4 cup melted butter
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- Butter for cooking

## Instructions

### Prepare Batter
1. In a large bowl, whisk together flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt
2. In another bowl, beat eggs then whisk in milk, melted butter, and vanilla
3. Pour wet ingredients into dry ingredients and stir until just combined

### Cook Pancakes  
4. Heat a griddle or large skillet over medium heat and brush with butter
5. Pour 1/4 cup batter for each pancake onto the hot griddle
6. Cook until bubbles form on surface and edges look set, about 2-3 minutes
7. Flip and cook until golden brown on other side, 1-2 minutes more

## Notes
- Don't overmix the batter - lumps are okay
- Let batter rest for 5 minutes before cooking for fluffier pancakes
- Serve immediately with maple syrup and butter
- Leftover pancakes can be frozen for up to 3 months
