### Ingredients

<RecipeIngredients>
  <>Ground Beef (1 lb)</>
  <>Taco Seasoning Packet (1 packet)</>
  <>Small Tortillas (corn or flour) (8 pieces)</>
  <>Shredded Lettuce (1 cup)</>
  <>Diced Tomatoes (1 cup)</>
  <>Diced Onion (1/2 cup)</>
  <>Shredded Cheddar Cheese (1 cup)</>
  <>Sour Cream (1/2 cup)</>
  <>Fresh Cilantro, chopped (1/4 cup)</>
  <>Limes, cut into wedges (2 pieces)</>
  <>Hot Sauce (optional)</>
</RecipeIngredients>

### Instructions

<RecipeInstructions>
  <RecipeInstructions.Section title="Prepare Beef">
    <RecipeInstructions.Step>
      Cook <strong>Ground Beef</strong> in a large skillet over medium-high heat, breaking it up as it cooks.
    </RecipeInstructions.Step>
    <RecipeInstructions.Step>
      Drain fat and add <strong>Taco Seasoning</strong> with <strong>2/3 cup water</strong>. Simmer for <strong>5 minutes</strong>.
    </RecipeInstructions.Step>
  </RecipeInstructions.Section>
  <RecipeInstructions.Section title="Assemble Tacos">
    <RecipeInstructions.Step>
      Warm <strong>Tortillas</strong> in a dry skillet or microwave.
    </RecipeInstructions.Step>
    <RecipeInstructions.Step>
      Fill each tortilla with <strong>Beef Mixture</strong>.
    </RecipeInstructions.Step>
    <RecipeInstructions.Step>
      Top with <strong>Lettuce</strong>, <strong>Tomatoes</strong>, <strong>Onion</strong>, <strong>Cheese</strong>, and <strong>Sour Cream</strong>.
    </RecipeInstructions.Step>
    <RecipeInstructions.Step>
      Garnish with <strong>Cilantro</strong> and serve with <strong>Lime Wedges</strong> and <strong>Hot Sauce</strong>.
    </RecipeInstructions.Step>
  </RecipeInstructions.Section>
</RecipeInstructions>
