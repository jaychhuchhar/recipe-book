### Ingredients

<RecipeIngredients>
  <>Mixed Berries (fresh or frozen) (1 cup)</>
  <>Spinach (1 cup)</>
  <>Banana (1 piece)</>
  <>Almond Milk (1 cup)</>
  <>Chia Seeds (optional) (1 tbsp)</>
</RecipeIngredients>

### Instructions

<RecipeInstructions>
  <RecipeInstructionsSection title="Make Smoothie">
    <RecipeInstructionsStep>
      Blend all ingredients until smooth. Serve immediately.
    </RecipeInstructionsStep>
  </RecipeInstructionsSection>
</RecipeInstructions>
