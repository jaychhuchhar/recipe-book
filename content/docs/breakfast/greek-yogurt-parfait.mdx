### Ingredients

<RecipeIngredients>
  <>Greek Yogurt (1 cup)</>
  <>Mixed Berries (1/2 cup)</>
  <>Granola (1/4 cup)</>
  <>Honey (1 tbsp)</>
</RecipeIngredients>

### Instructions

<RecipeInstructions>
  <RecipeInstructionsSection title="Assemble Parfait">
    <RecipeInstructionsStep>
      Layer <strong>Yogurt</strong>, <strong>Berries</strong>, and <strong>Granola</strong> in a glass.
    </RecipeInstructionsStep>
    <RecipeInstructionsStep>
      Drizzle with <strong>Honey</strong> and enjoy!
    </RecipeInstructionsStep>
  </RecipeInstructionsSection>
</RecipeInstructions>

### Notes

<RecipeNotes>
  <>Use plain Greek yogurt for lower sugar content</>
  <>Can substitute honey with maple syrup</>
  <>Make multiple servings ahead for quick breakfast</>
</RecipeNotes>
