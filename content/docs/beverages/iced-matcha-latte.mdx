### Ingredients

<RecipeIngredients>
  <>Matcha Powder (1 tsp)</>
  <>Hot Water (1/4 cup)</>
  <>Milk (dairy or plant-based) (3/4 cup)</>
  <>Ice</>
  <>Honey or Maple Syrup (optional) (1 tsp)</>
</RecipeIngredients>

### Instructions

<RecipeInstructions>
  <RecipeInstructionsSection title="Prepare Matcha Latte">
    <RecipeInstructionsStep>
      Whisk <strong>Matcha</strong> with <strong>Hot Water</strong> until smooth.
    </RecipeInstructionsStep>
    <RecipeInstructionsStep>
      Add <strong>Milk</strong> and <strong>Sweetener</strong>. Pour over <strong>Ice</strong> and enjoy.
    </RecipeInstructionsStep>
  </RecipeInstructionsSection>
</RecipeInstructions>

### Notes

<RecipeNotes>
  <>Use ceremonial grade matcha for best flavor</>
  <>Whisk vigorously to avoid lumps</>
  <>Can be made hot by using warm milk instead of ice</>
</RecipeNotes>
