### Ingredients

<RecipeIngredients>
  <>Large Whole Wheat Tortilla (1 piece)</>
  <>Hummus (1/4 cup)</>
  <>Mixed Veggies (carrot, cucumber, bell pepper) (1/2 cup)</>
  <>Handful of Greens</>
</RecipeIngredients>

### Instructions

<RecipeInstructions>
  <RecipeInstructions.Section title="Assemble Wrap">
    <RecipeInstructions.Step>
      Spread <strong>Hummus</strong> on <strong>Tortilla</strong>.
    </RecipeInstructions.Step>
    <RecipeInstructions.Step>
      Add <strong>Veggies</strong> and <strong>Greens</strong>. Roll up and slice.
    </RecipeInstructions.Step>
  </RecipeInstructions.Section>
</RecipeInstructions>

### Notes

<RecipeNotes>

  <>Use any combination of fresh vegetables</>
