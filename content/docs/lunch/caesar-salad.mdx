### Ingredients

<RecipeIngredients>
  <>Garlic, minced (3 cloves)</>
  <>Anchovy Fillets (optional) (2 pieces)</>
  <>Lemon Juice (1/4 cup)</>
  <>Dijon Mustard (1 tsp)</>
  <>Olive Oil (1/2 cup)</>
  <>Grated Parmesan Cheese (1/4 cup)</>
  <>Egg Yolk (1 piece)</>
  <>Romaine Lettuce, chopped (2 large heads)</>
  <>Parmesan Cheese, shaved (1/2 cup)</>
  <>Croutons (1 cup)</>
  <>Salt and Black Pepper to taste</>
</RecipeIngredients>

### Instructions

<RecipeInstructions>
  <RecipeInstructionsSection title="Make Dressing">
    <RecipeInstructionsStep>
      In a small bowl, mash <strong>Garlic</strong> and <strong>Anchovies</strong> into a paste.
    </RecipeInstructionsStep>
    <RecipeInstructionsStep>
      Whisk in <strong>Lemon Juice</strong>, <strong>Dijon Mustard</strong>, and <strong>Egg Yolk</strong>.
    </RecipeInstructionsStep>
    <RecipeInstructionsStep>
      Slowly drizzle in <strong>Olive Oil</strong> while whisking to emulsify.
    </RecipeInstructionsStep>
    <RecipeInstructionsStep>
      Stir in <strong>Grated Parmesan</strong>, season with <strong>Salt</strong> and <strong>Pepper</strong>.
    </RecipeInstructionsStep>
  </RecipeInstructionsSection>
  <RecipeInstructionsSection title="Assemble Salad">
    <RecipeInstructionsStep>
      Place chopped <strong>Romaine Lettuce</strong> in a large bowl.
    </RecipeInstructionsStep>
    <RecipeInstructionsStep>
      Pour <strong>Dressing</strong> over lettuce and toss well.
    </RecipeInstructionsStep>
    <RecipeInstructionsStep>
      Top with <strong>Shaved Parmesan</strong>, <strong>Croutons</strong>, and freshly ground <strong>Black Pepper</strong>.
    </RecipeInstructionsStep>
  </RecipeInstructionsSection>
</RecipeInstructions>

### Notes

<RecipeNotes>
  <>For food safety, use pasteurized eggs or skip the raw egg</>
  <>Make croutons by toasting cubed bread with olive oil and garlic</>
  <>Dressing can be made ahead and stored in refrigerator for up to 3 days</>
</RecipeNotes>
