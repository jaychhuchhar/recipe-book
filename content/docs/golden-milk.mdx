## Ingredients
- 1 cup milk (dairy or plant-based)
- 1/2 tsp turmeric
- 1/4 tsp cinnamon
- Pinch of black pepper
- 1 tsp honey or maple syrup

## Instructions
1. Heat milk and spices in a saucepan until warm.
2. Sweeten to taste. Whisk and serve.
