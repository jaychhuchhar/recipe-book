## Ingredients
- 1 large whole wheat tortilla
- 1/4 cup hummus
- 1/2 cup mixed veggies (carrot, cucumber, bell pepper)
- Handful of greens

## Instructions
1. Spread hummus on tortilla.
2. Add veggies and greens. Roll up and slice.
