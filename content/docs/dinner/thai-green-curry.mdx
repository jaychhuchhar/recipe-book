### Ingredients

<RecipeIngredients>
  <>Green Curry Paste (2-3 tbsp)</>
  <>Coconut Milk (1 can, 14 oz)</>
  <>Chicken Breast or Tofu, cubed (1 lb)</>
  <>Eggplant, cubed (1 piece)</>
  <>Bell Pepper, sliced (1 piece)</>
  <>Green Beans, trimmed (1/2 cup)</>
  <>Bamboo Shoots (1/4 cup)</>
  <>Fish Sauce (or soy sauce) (2 tbsp)</>
  <>Brown Sugar (1 tbsp)</>
  <>Thai Basil Leaves (3-4 pieces)</>
  <>Kaffir Lime Leaves (2 pieces)</>
  <>Red Chili, sliced (1 piece)</>
  <>Jasmine Rice for serving</>
</RecipeIngredients>

### Instructions

<RecipeInstructions>
  <RecipeInstructions.Section title="Prepare Curry Base">
    <RecipeInstructions.Step>
      Heat <strong>1/2 cup of Coconut Milk</strong> in a large pan over medium heat.
    </RecipeInstructions.Step>
    <RecipeInstructions.Step>
      Add <strong>Green Curry Paste</strong> and cook for <strong>2 minutes</strong> until fragrant.
    </RecipeInstructions.Step>
    <RecipeInstructions.Step>
      Add <strong>Chicken/Tofu</strong> and cook until nearly done.
    </RecipeInstructions.Step>
  </RecipeInstructions.Section>
  <RecipeInstructions.Section title="Add Vegetables">
    <RecipeInstructions.Step>
      Pour in remaining <strong>Coconut Milk</strong> and bring to a simmer.
    </RecipeInstructions.Step>
    <RecipeInstructions.Step>
      Add <strong>Eggplant</strong> and cook for <strong>5 minutes</strong>.
    </RecipeInstructions.Step>
    <RecipeInstructions.Step>
      Add <strong>Bell Pepper</strong>, <strong>Green Beans</strong>, and <strong>Bamboo Shoots</strong>. Cook <strong>5 more minutes</strong>.
    </RecipeInstructions.Step>
    <RecipeInstructions.Step>
      Stir in <strong>Fish Sauce</strong>, <strong>Brown Sugar</strong>, and <strong>Lime Leaves</strong>.
    </RecipeInstructions.Step>
    <RecipeInstructions.Step>
      Garnish with <strong>Thai Basil</strong> and sliced <strong>Chili</strong>. Serve over <strong>Jasmine Rice</strong>.
    </RecipeInstructions.Step>
  </RecipeInstructions.Section>
</RecipeInstructions>

### Notes

<RecipeNotes>
  <>Adjust curry paste amount based on desired spice level</>
  <>Thai basil has a distinct flavor; regular basil can substitute if needed</>
  <>Add vegetables in order of cooking time - harder vegetables first</>
</RecipeNotes>
