### Ingredients

<RecipeIngredients>
  <>Cooked Quinoa or Brown Rice (1 cup)</>
  <>Chickpeas, drained (1 cup)</>
  <>Broccoli Florets (1 cup)</>
  <>Sweet Potato, cubed (1 cup)</>
  <>Avocado, sliced (1 piece)</>
  <>Mixed Greens (1 cup)</>
  <>Tahini (2 tbsp)</>
  <>Lemon Juice (1 tbsp)</>
  <>Salt & Pepper to taste</>
</RecipeIngredients>

### Instructions

<RecipeInstructions>
  <RecipeInstructions.Section title="Prepare Vegetables">
    <RecipeInstructions.Step>
      Roast <strong>Sweet Potato</strong> and <strong>Broccoli</strong> at <strong>400°F (200°C)</strong> for <strong>20 minutes</strong>.
    </RecipeInstructions.Step>
  </RecipeInstructions.Section>
  <RecipeInstructions.Section title="Assemble Bowl">
    <RecipeInstructions.Step>
      Arrange all ingredients in a bowl.
    </RecipeInstructions.Step>
    <RecipeInstructions.Step>
      Whisk <strong>Tahini</strong>, <strong>Lemon Juice</strong>, <strong>Salt</strong>, and <strong>Pepper</strong> for dressing. Drizzle over bowl.
    </RecipeInstructions.Step>
  </RecipeInstructions.Section>
</RecipeInstructions>

### Notes

<RecipeNotes>
  <>Can substitute any seasonal vegetables</>
  <>Add hemp seeds or nuts for extra protein</>
  <>Make components ahead for quick assembly</>
</RecipeNotes>
