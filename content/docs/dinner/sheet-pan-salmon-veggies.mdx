### Ingredients

<RecipeIngredients>
  <>Salmon Fillets (2 pieces)</>
  <>Cherry Tomatoes (1 cup)</>
  <>Broccoli Florets (1 cup)</>
  <>Bell Pepper, sliced (1 piece)</>
  <>Olive Oil (1 tbsp)</>
  <>Lemon, sliced (1 piece)</>
  <>Salt & Pepper to taste</>
</RecipeIngredients>

### Instructions

<RecipeInstructions>
  <RecipeInstructions.Section title="Prepare Sheet Pan">
    <RecipeInstructions.Step>
      Preheat oven to <strong>425°F (220°C)</strong>.
    </RecipeInstructions.Step>
    <RecipeInstructions.Step>
      Arrange <strong>Salmon</strong> and <strong>Veggies</strong> on a sheet pan.
    </RecipeInstructions.Step>
    <RecipeInstructions.Step>
      Drizzle with <strong>Olive Oil</strong>, season with <strong>Salt</strong> and <strong>Pepper</strong>.
    </RecipeInstructions.Step>
    <RecipeInstructions.Step>
      Top salmon with <strong>Lemon Slices</strong>.
    </RecipeInstructions.Step>
    <RecipeInstructions.Step>
      Bake for <strong>15-20 minutes</strong> until salmon is cooked through.
    </RecipeInstructions.Step>
  </RecipeInstructions.Section>
</RecipeInstructions>

### Notes

<RecipeNotes>
  <>Salmon is done when it flakes easily with a fork</>
  <>Can substitute vegetables based on preference</>
  <>Line pan with parchment paper for easy cleanup</>
</RecipeNotes>
