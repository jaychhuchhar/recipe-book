## Ingredients
- 1 lb boneless chicken breast, cubed
- 1/2 cup plain yogurt
- 2 tbsp lemon juice
- 2 tsp garam masala
- 1 tsp turmeric
- 1 tsp cumin
- 1 tsp paprika
- 1 onion, chopped
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tbsp ginger, grated
- 1 can (14 oz) tomato sauce
- 1/2 cup heavy cream
- 2 tbsp butter
- Salt & pepper to taste

## Instructions
1. Marinate chicken in yogurt, lemon juice, and half the spices for 30 minutes.
2. Saut√© onion, garlic, and ginger in butter until soft.
3. Add remaining spices, cook 1 minute.
4. Add chicken and cook until browned.
5. Stir in tomato sauce, simmer 20 minutes.
6. Add cream, simmer 10 more minutes. Serve with rice or naan.
