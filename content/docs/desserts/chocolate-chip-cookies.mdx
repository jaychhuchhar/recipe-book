### Ingredients

<RecipeIngredients>
  <>Room Temperature Butter (1 Cup)</>
  <>Powdered Sugar (1.5 Cup)</>
  <>All Purpose Flour (3 Cup)</>
  <>Egg (2 Nos)</>
  <>Baking Powder (2 Tsp)</>
  <>Salt (0.5 Tsp)</>
  <>Vanilla Extract (2 Tsp)</>
  <>Chocolate Chips (1 Heartful)</>
</RecipeIngredients>

### Instructions

<InstructionsContainer>
  <InstructionsSection title="Prepare Dry Ingredients">
    <InstructionStep stepNumber={1}>
      In a mixing bowl, add All <strong>Purpose</strong> <strong>Flour</strong>, <strong>Baking</strong> <strong>Powder</strong>, and <strong>Salt</strong> and mix thoroughly.
    </InstructionStep>
  </InstructionsSection>
  <InstructionsSection title="Prepare Wet Ingredients">
    <InstructionStep stepNumber={2}>
      In a mixing bowl, add <strong>Butter</strong> and <strong>Sugar</strong>, mix it well.
    </InstructionStep>

    <InstructionStep stepNumber={3}>
      Add 1 egg at a time mixing it thoroughly.
    </InstructionStep>

    <InstructionStep stepNumber={4}>
      Add <strong>Vanilla</strong> <strong>Extract</strong> and mix well.
    </InstructionStep>
  </InstructionsSection>
  <InstructionsSection title="Combine and Bake">
    <InstructionStep stepNumber={5}>
      Add Dry Mix, 1/3 at a time mixing it thoroughly.
    </InstructionStep>

    <InstructionStep stepNumber={6}>
      Once everything is mixed properly, add <strong>Chocolate</strong> <strong>Chips</strong> and fold in the mixture.
    </InstructionStep>

    <InstructionStep stepNumber={7}>
      Make medium sized cookies and put them on <strong>baking</strong> paper over a <strong>baking</strong> tray.
    </InstructionStep>

    <InstructionStep stepNumber={8}>
      Preheat the oven at 180â„ƒ and bake 5 to 6 medium cookies at a time for 15 min to 17 min.
    </InstructionStep>
  </InstructionsSection>
</InstructionsContainer>
