## Ingredients
- 8 oz spaghetti
- 3 cups vegetable broth
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 lemon, zested and juiced
- 1/4 cup cream or coconut milk
- 1/4 cup grated parmesan
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- Salt & pepper to taste

## Instructions
1. In a large pot, saut√© garlic in olive oil.
2. Add spaghetti, broth, lemon zest, and juice. Bring to a boil.
3. Simmer until pasta is cooked and liquid is mostly absorbed.
4. Stir in cream and parmesan. Season to taste.
