## Ingredients
- 1 slice whole grain bread
- 1/2 ripe avocado
- 1 egg
- Salt, pepper, chili flakes

## Instructions
1. Toast bread. Mash avocado on top.
2. Cook egg as desired (fried or poached) and place on toast.
3. Season and enjoy!
