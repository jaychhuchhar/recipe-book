## Ingredients
- 1 cup Greek yogurt
- 1/2 cup mixed berries
- 1/4 cup granola
- 1 tbsp honey

## Instructions
1. Layer yogurt, berries, and granola in a glass.
2. Drizzle with honey and enjoy!
