## Ingredients
- 1 tsp matcha powder
- 1/4 cup hot water
- 3/4 cup milk (dairy or plant-based)
- Ice
- 1 tsp honey or maple syrup (optional)

## Instructions
1. Whisk matcha with hot water until smooth.
2. Add milk and sweetener. Pour over ice and enjoy.
