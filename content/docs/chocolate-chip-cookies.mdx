### Cooking Time

- **Prep Time**: 30 Minutes
- **Cook Time**: 1 Hour
- **Total Time**: 1.5 Hour

### Servings

- **Serves**: 16

### Ingredients

- **Room Temperature Butter** (1 Cup)
- **Powdered Sugar** (1.5 Cup)
- **All Purpose Flour** (3 Cup)
- **Egg** (2 Nos)
- **Baking Powder** (2 Tsp)
- **Salt** (0.5 Tsp)
- **Vanilla Extract** (2 Tsp)
- **Chocolate Chips** (1 Heartful)

### Instructions

- **Step 1**: **Dry Mix**: In a mixing bowl, add **All Purpose Flour**, **Baking Powder**,and **Salt** and mix thoroughly.
- **Step 2**: **Wet Mix:**: In a mixing Bowl, add **Butter** and **Sugar**, mix it well.
- **Step 3**: Add 1 **egg** at a time mixing it thoroughly.
- **Step 4**: Add **Vanila Extract** and mix well.
- **Step 5**: Add **Dry Mix**, 1/3 at a time mixing it thoroughly.
- **Step 6**: Once everything is mixed properly, add **Chocolate Chips** and fold in the mixure.
- **Step 7**: Make medium sized cookies and put them on baking paper over a baking tray.
- **Step 8**: Preheat the oven at **180â„ƒ** and bake **5** to **6** medium cookies at a time for **15 min** to **17 min**.

### Notes

- **Storage**: Do not refrigerate. Keep it in cool, air tight and dry place.
- Use hand for mixing for better result.
- Use rotating plate for even cooking if using conventional oven in microwave.
